Key Impact Factors on Mental Health

Economic Stress

High unemployment and financial instability contribute to rising depression rates globally.

Social Isolation

Loneliness and lack of social support are linked to increased anxiety and depressive disorders.

Access to Care

Limited mental health services prevent early diagnosis and treatment in many regions.
